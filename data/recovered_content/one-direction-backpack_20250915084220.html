Cleaning Tips for Long-Lasting Use

The backpacks are made of polyester, which is easy to clean11. Just use a damp cloth and some mild soap to wipe off dirt or stains. Don’t use strong cleaners or put it in the washing machine. It could mess up the designs and the backpack’s shape11.
